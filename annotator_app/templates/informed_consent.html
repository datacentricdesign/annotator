Informed consent

You are being invited to participate in a PhD study to explore the potential of crowdsourcing techniques for Strava running data interpretation.

Agreement to Participate

Your participation is completely voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time.

To take this survey, you must be:

• At least 18 years old

• Strava user who has 5 run records in recent 2 months

What will you do?

You will be invited to participate in a longitudinal study and do the following tasks:

• The first study: upload 1) screenshots of the recent two-month running route map(figure1) and 2) running frequency visualization (figure 2) showing at least 5 runs in the recent 2 months.

• The second study: upload the screenshots same as the first study. Then, you will be asked to annotate your running experiences by reflecting on visualisations and answering prepared questions.

If you meet the requirements of the first study, you will receive our invitation for the second study and be paid until completing all parts of the study. If not, you will be paid immediately after finishing the first study. Each study will be approved within 21 days. The total task will take you approximately 20 minutes to complete (5mins+15mins).
