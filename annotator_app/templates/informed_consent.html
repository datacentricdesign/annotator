Are you the one we are looking for?

You are being invited to scientific research around Strava running experiences. Your participation is completely voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time. To participate in this survey, we expect you are:
1. at least 18 years old
2. a Strava user who has 5 running records in recent 2 months

What task will you do?

You will be invited to do the following tasks:

  • Upload screenshots of the Strava activity records,including running route map (in recent two-months) and training calendar (recent two-months and at least 3 activity records).
  • Then, please reflect on the uploaded screenshots and answer questions around your running experiences in text format.​

                                             Running route map                                                  ​         ​                                                      Training calendar

We cannot pay you if you do not meet those requriments even if you complete the whole tasks. This study will be approved within 21 days. The total task will take you approximately 15 minutes maximum to complete.

What data will be collected?

We will collect the following data for research: 

  • Strava App screenshots: running route map  and training calendar
  • Overall information about specific runs, including timestamps, moving time, distance etc.
  • Annotations of running experience in text format (a list of questions around running experiences will be provided for you to reflect upon collected screenshots)​

How do we protect your data?

We believe there are no known risks associated with this research study. However, as with any online related activity, the risk of a breach is always possible. We highlight the most probable risk is you might take wrong screenshots which includes your identifying information (e.g., name, profile image). We are protecting your data and minimizing the risks in the following ways:

  •  We design this survey to be anonymous, where all identifying information is removed and replaced with your Prolific ID. 
  •  We will provide clear and detailed instructions to take correct screenshots, and exclude your personal information. Please follow the instructions strictly. 
  • If any identifying information is collected, we will delete it immediately before storing. 
  •  We will store all data on a password-protected, encrypted computer and server, and data will only be accessed by our research group members.
